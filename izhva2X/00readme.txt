The dictionary file(s) in this directory contain(s) words and expressions collected en passant by the Izhva Komi Documentation Project (Freiburg, München, Syktyvkar). Note that the project does not intend to carry out systematic lexicographic research.

Metainfo (data sources)
*Sizjabsk_school_2014 is a wordlist provided to us by the Secondary school in Sizjabsk, Ižemskij rajon on 31 March, 2014
*fieldnotes_MR_2014 are words taken from Michael Rießler's fieldnotes during our stay in the Ižemskij rajon in March 2014
